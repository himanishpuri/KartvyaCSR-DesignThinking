About Hope Foundation

Hope Foundation was established in 2005 with a mission to provide quality education and healthcare to underprivileged children in rural India. Over the past two decades, we have extended our reach to over 200 villages across 15 states, impacting over 50,000 children annually.

Key Focus Areas
  • Primary education in rural communities
  • Healthcare and nutrition for children
  • Digital literacy programs
  • Teacher training and capacity building
  • Infrastructure development for schools
Achievements
  • Established 120+ learning centers in remote villages
  • Trained 2000+ teachers in modern teaching methodologies
  • Achieved 95% retention rate in schools we support
  • Distributed learning materials to 100,000+ students
Current Projects

About Green Earth Initiative

Green Earth Initiative is a leading environmental conservation organization focused on restoring India's green cover and promoting sustainable farming practices. Founded in 2008, we've been working with local communities to protect natural resources and build climate resilience.

Key Focus Areas
  • Reforestation and tree plantation drives
  • Watershed management and rainwater harvesting
  • Sustainable agriculture training
  • Climate change adaptation for farmers
  • Environmental education in schools
Achievements
  • Planted over 3 million trees across 6 states
  • Restored 120+ water bodies in drought-prone regions
  • Trained 25,000+ farmers in sustainable farming techniques
  • Established 50+ seed banks for indigenous crop varieties
Current Projects
Million Tree Mission

Planting one million native tree species in degraded forest lands of Western Ghats.

72% Complete
Farmer Climate Resilience

Helping 5,000 small farmers adapt to changing climate conditions with sustainable methods.

About Shakti Foundation

Shakti Foundation has been at the forefront of women's empowerment in India since 2010. We focus on economic independence, skill development, and entrepreneurship opportunities for women in underserved communities, with a special focus on rural and semi-urban areas.

Key Focus Areas
  • Vocational training and skill development
  • Microfinance and self-help groups
  • Women entrepreneurship development
  • Digital literacy for women
  • Gender sensitization programs
Achievements
  • Trained 30,000+ women in various vocational skills
  • Established 2,500+ self-help groups across 8 states
  • Supported 5,000+ women entrepreneurs with microloans
  • Achieved 85% business sustainability rate among supported ventures
Current Projects
Digital Sakhi Program

Training 10,000 women as digital literacy ambassadors in their communities.

45% Complete
Rural Enterprise Hub

Creating 50 enterprise hubs to support women-owned businesses with resources and mentoring.
