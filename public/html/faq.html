General Questions

CSR Connect is an innovative platform designed to bridge the gap between NGOs and Corporates for effective CSR collaboration. Our platform enables NGOs to showcase their projects and attract corporate funding, while helping companies find credible NGO partners aligned with their CSR goals. We simplify the process of CSR implementation, reporting, and impact assessment.

For NGOs: We provide a platform to showcase projects, connect with potential corporate funders, streamline reporting processes, and gain visibility among companies seeking CSR partners.

For Corporates: We offer access to a curated network of verified NGOs, simplified project selection aligned with CSR objectives, comprehensive reporting tools, impact tracking, and regulatory compliance support.

CSR Connect operates on a freemium model. Basic registration and access to core features are free for both NGOs and corporates. Premium features, including advanced analytics, priority support, and enhanced visibility, require a subscription. We also offer enterprise plans for larger organizations with specific needs. Visit our pricing page for detailed information.

We implement a rigorous verification process for all organizations joining our platform. NGOs must provide valid registration documents, tax exemption certificates, annual reports, and financial statements. Corporates are verified through their corporate identification numbers, CSR policy documents, and other relevant credentials. Our team conducts thorough checks to ensure the authenticity and credibility of all platform members.

For NGOs

To register your NGO on CSR Connect, you'll need:

  • NGO registration certificate
  • 80G certification (if applicable)
  • FCRA registration (if applicable)
  • PAN card details
  • Last three years' annual reports
  • Financial statements or audit reports
  • Organization profile and mission statement
  • Board member details
  • Past project documentation (optional but recommended)

To increase your NGO's visibility on CSR Connect:

  1. Complete your profile with comprehensive information about your organization's mission, impact, and capabilities
  2. Showcase your past projects with clear objectives, outcomes, and impact metrics
  3. Upload high-quality images and videos of your work
  4. Regularly update your project portfolio with new initiatives
  5. Maintain detailed documentation of all compliance certificates
  6. Request and display testimonials from previous partners
  7. Participate in platform events and webinars
  8. Utilize our project proposal templates to create compelling funding requests

While funding preferences vary among corporates, projects that typically attract CSR funding include:

  • Education and skill development initiatives
  • Healthcare and sanitation projects
  • Environmental sustainability programs
  • Women's empowerment and gender equality
  • Rural development projects
  • Projects aligned with Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)
  • Programs with measurable outcomes and clear impact metrics
  • Initiatives with potential for employee engagement
  • Projects in geographies where the corporate has operations
  • Innovations addressing pressing social challenges

The key is to clearly articulate how your project aligns with potential corporate partners' CSR focus areas and how you'll measure and report on outcomes.

Account & Registration

The verification process typically takes:

  • Basic verification: 1-2 business days
  • Comprehensive verification: 3-5 business days

The timeline may vary depending on the completeness of submitted documentation and our verification team's current workload. You'll receive status updates via email throughout the process, and you can always check your verification status in your account dashboard. We prioritize thoroughness in our verification process to maintain the quality and credibility of organizations on our platform.

Yes, CSR Connect supports multi-user access with role-based permissions:

  • Primary Administrator: Full access to all features, account settings, and user management
  • Finance Manager: Access to financial reports, fund tracking, and expense documentation
  • Project Manager: Project creation, updates, and team coordination
  • Content Manager: Ability to update profiles, upload media, and manage public-facing content
  • Report Viewer: Read-only access to reports and dashboards
  • Field Agent: Mobile access for data collection and implementation updates

The number of user accounts available depends on your subscription plan. The primary account administrator can add users and set appropriate permission levels through the User Management section in Settings.

To reset your password:

  1. Click on "Login" at the top of the page
  2. Select "Forgot Password" below the login form
  3. Enter your registered email address
  4. Check your email for a password reset link (valid for 24 hours)
  5. Click the link and create a new password

To update account information:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner
  3. Select "Account Settings" from the dropdown menu
  4. Navigate to the relevant section (Personal Information, Organization Details, etc.)
  5. Make your changes and click "Save"

For security-sensitive changes, you may be required to verify your identity through email confirmation or by providing additional documentation.

Legal & Compliance

CSR Connect prioritizes data privacy and security through:

  • End-to-end encryption for all sensitive data
  • Compliance with GDPR and applicable data protection regulations
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability testing
  • Strict access controls and role-based permissions
  • Secure document storage with encryption at rest
  • Two-factor authentication for account access
  • Data retention policies that protect sensitive information
  • Transparent privacy policy detailing how your data is used
  • No sharing of personal or organizational data with third parties without consent
  • Regular backups to prevent data loss

For more details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

CSR Connect facilitates the following contractual agreements:

  1. Platform Terms of Service: Governs the use of CSR Connect by all users
  2. CSR Project Agreement Template: Customizable agreement between corporates and NGOs for specific projects
  3. Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA): Optional template for sensitive discussions during project exploration
  4. Memorandum of Understanding (MoU): Template for initial partnership establishment
  5. Fund Disbursement Agreement: Terms for milestone-based fund releases
  6. Reporting and Monitoring Framework: Establishes expectations for project updates and verification

While we provide these templates to facilitate partnerships, we recommend review by your legal team before finalization. CSR Connect serves as a platform connecting parties but is not itself a party to agreements between NGOs and corporates.

CSR Connect assists with Companies Act compliance through:

  • Verification of NGO eligibility under Section 135 requirements
  • CSR-1 form verification for implementation partners
  • Structured documentation of CSR policy implementation
  • Automated generation of annual CSR reports in the required format
  • Impact assessment documentation for projects exceeding ₹1 crore
  • Board report annexures with all mandated disclosures
  • Tracking of 2% average net profit allocation compliance
  • Documentation of unspent CSR amount transfers to specified funds
  • Regulatory updates and compliance alerts
  • Audit trail maintenance for all CSR activities

Our platform is regularly updated to reflect the latest amendments to the Companies Act and CSR Rules, ensuring your compliance stays current with regulatory requirements.
